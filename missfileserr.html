Have your files gone missing? Here is some ways to fix them:

- Contact PL Management

- Restart

- Remove any and all files relating to "warnings"

- Look in TDUNI in TDUNI/Content/INKDU/Drives/ for your files.

Please notice that files from TDUNI simulation may be sent to your file, possibly corrupting some.

If anything, it is OK to restart. Usually, files are backed up every Monday.

In rare circumstances, the cloud has the abitity to corrupt.
